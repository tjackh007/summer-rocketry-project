# Project Plan

## Purpose

This project is intended to provide hands-on aerospace engineering experience through the build, simulation, and launch planning of a beginner model rocket.

## Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if:

- The rocket is fully assembled
- The rocket is modeled in OpenRocket
- At least two motor options are simulated
- Final mass and CG are measured
- A launch card is created
- A final project summary is completed

## Stretch Goal

Complete a safe launch through a recognized club or supervised launch environment and compare actual performance against simulated predictions.
